How to Play

Trick-Taking: Winner of the last trick leads the next (enemy leads first). When leading, play first — the enemy responds. When following, match suit with higher rank to win.

Suits:

  • Hearts — +mult modifier
  • Diamonds — +chip modifier
  • Clubs — +direct damage
  • Spades — Reveal enemy card
  • Stars ⭐ — Universal modifier (applies to all suits)

Off-Suit Tactical Retreats: Playing off-suit can't win, but grants bonuses: Foundation = triple bait shield, Heart = modifier boost + heal, Crown = store damage for next Crown win.

Sacrifice System: Each lost trick charges Sacrifice (max 3). At 3 charges, your next WIN gets a ×2.5 Vengeance multiplier! Losing strategically is powerful.

Crown Gambit: Losing on Crown row stores bonus damage. Next Crown WIN adds stored damage as bonus chips.

Encounter Formats:

  • 🏟️ Gauntlet — 5 quick enemies, 1 trick each
  • 🧩 Puzzle — Win exactly 2 of 4 tricks
  • 🎲 Bid — Predict your wins for bonus rewards

Rows: Crown (×mult, Surge: ×2 next win), Heart (+chips, Surge: CHIP DOUBLER — all chip mods ×2 for encounter!), Shield (defend, Surge: SHIELDBREAKER — next win converts shield to damage!).

Encounter Rules: Each enemy has a unique rule that changes how you play — suit restrictions, row locks, rank decay, damage gates, and more. Read the rule banner at the top of each encounter!

Burn Pile: Discarded cards go to the burn pile. View with 🔥 Burn button. Scavenge to pull cards back (costs Embers). Pyre keyword gets +chips per burn pile card.

Embers: Earn Embers from combat. Visit the Forge on the map to upgrade Flame → Inferno modifiers.

Ascension: After victory, unlock higher Ascension levels (up to 20) for greater challenge and rewards.

Mobile: Long-press a card to inspect. Swipe up on a selected card to play.

Keys: 1/2/3 for rows, Enter to play, M for matrix, B for burn pile.
